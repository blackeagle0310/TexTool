TexTool {0}
A tool (and a mini-library) to convert CM3D2 TEX files.

Usage:

{1}.exe <files or folders>

You can also drag-and-drop folder/files onto the tool.

How it works:

All provided .tex files are converted to .png.
All provided valid image files (png, jpg, etc.) are converted to PNG and saved as .tex version 1010.

All output is put into the "output" folder.
